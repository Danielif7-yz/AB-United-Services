About Us

ABUS is equipped with seasoned & knowledge based highly skilled Nigerians with wealth of experience and technical capabilities to serve the oil and gas and the non-oil and gas sector. We provide the most reliable, transparent and quality services tailored to costumer’s specifications and expectation.

AB UNITED SERVICES LIMITED is a Company with over 13 years of experience in both the oil and gas and the non- oil and gas sector of the Nigerian Industries. The Company was incorporated in Nigeria in 2003 with the Registration No: RC: 479114 as a Limited Liability Company.


Since in business, ABUS has successfully carried out services in its field of operations in onshore and offshore pipelines/storage facility maintenance services (corrosion control and protection engineering– sand/grit blasting and painting /coating of storage tanks and vessels etc, Plant Maintenance, Rehabilitations, Nondestructive testing, Hydro testing, Mechanical rotating, Welding and Fabrication, Mechanical, Electrical, Civil Engineering and Environmental services, to both producing and the non-producing companies in the oil and gas sectors and the non oil and gas sector.

We have well experienced technical team ranging from Administrators, Accountants, Planners, Engineering (Civil, Quantity Surveyors, Mechanical, Electrical and Chemical Engineers) and Researchers, Statisticians, Analyst, Environmentalist, etc. These form our technical team. We have access to all needed equipment for our jobs.

We are registered contractors to Port Harcourt Refinery (PHRC), Eleme Petrochemical (EPCL), Warri Refinery Company, Nigeria Agip Oil Company (NAOC), Niger Delta Development Commission(NDDC), NAPIMS, NETCO, KPSC, NGC, HYSON, TOTAL E&P, and have executed jobs successfuly with efficiency. These vindicate our expected capability to handle any job if called to do so.
